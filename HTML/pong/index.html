Ping Pong - By Prajwal Agrawal

Instructions:
Use the W and S keys to move your paddle.
You can also move your mouse.
If you are on a touch device, drag to move the
paddle.
Press ESC or click the button to pause/resume the game.
For 2 players:
W/S to move Player 1
Up/Down to move Player 2
tap and drag left side of screen to move Player 1
tap and drag right side of screen to move Player 2
